About Me

Hello! I'm Shreyash, a developer whose passion has evolved towards creating user-focused front-end experiences.

I started my software journey in the back-end, where I focused specifically on mastering the REST API module in Spring Boot. While I loved learning how data is structured and served, I realized I am much more passionate about what the user actually sees and interacts with.

Today, my primary focus is on Front-End Development and User Experience (UX). I am deeply interested in the science of scaling websites not just through code, but through design. I am currently studying how to create compelling websites that actually convert by applying proven, psychological UX principles.

My Current Focus & Skillset

  • Front-End & UX Design: Transitioning my focus to the front-end. I am learning how to design interfaces that actively defeat cognitive load, making it effortless for users to navigate and understand a website.
  • Conversion Rate Optimization: Studying how to scale a website's success by encouraging clicks and interactions naturally—without using shady tricks or dark patterns.
  • Data-Driven Design (A/B Testing): Learning how to validate design choices and improve conversion points through A/B and multivariate testing, ensuring decisions are based on real user data rather than guesswork.
  • REST API Integration: Thanks to my past experience building RESTful APIs with Spring Boot, I have a strong understanding of how the front-end communicates with the back-end, making me a more effective front-end developer.
